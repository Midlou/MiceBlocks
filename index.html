Mice Blocks - A Minecraft Resource Pack

Mice Blocks is a Minecraft Resource Pack with a simple and consistent design, inspired by oCd Texture Pack. Only using the Minecraft color palette, Mice Blocks is a smooth, easy on the eyes and lite alternative to the Vanilla texture. We are at Beta stage of development.

Sharp and Clean

Mice Blocks focuses on keeping textures simple and easy to identify.

When will it be finished?

Minecraft has a lot of textures. At moment, we have textures for blocks, interface and particles... Items, creatures, weapons and tools will be released later.


Wait! This is another copy of oCd Texture Pack!

As previously stated, Mice Blocks is inspired by oCd Texture pack, so there are similarities. it's important to highlight that every single texture in this pack was made from scratch.


Plans for the future?

A lot of plans! Animated textures, overlays textures, connected textures, block variants, all that good stuff.
